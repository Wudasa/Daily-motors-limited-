Our Story

Est. 2016, Nairobi

We Don't Just Sell Cars.
We Build Trust.

Daily Motors Ltd was born out of frustration with the traditional car buying experience — one full of hidden fees, pushy salespeople, and questionable vehicle histories. We set out to change that.

Starting with just 8 cars on a small lot in Nairobi, we have grown into one of the most trusted dealerships in Kenya, with over 500 satisfied clients and 120+ vehicles in stock at any time.

Our promise is simple: every car we sell is thoroughly inspected, honestly priced, and backed by our personal guarantee of quality.

What We Stand For

OUR VALUES

01
Transparency
No hidden fees. No misleading descriptions. What you see is exactly what you get — every vehicle listed with honest, complete information.
02
Quality
Every car undergoes a rigorous multi-point inspection before it reaches our showroom. We reject vehicles that don't meet our standards — no exceptions.
03
Community
We are Nairobi through and through. We reinvest in our community, employ locally, and take pride in being a brand Kenyans can genuinely trust.
04
Service
Our relationship doesn't end at the sale. We offer post-purchase support, refer trusted mechanics, and follow up to ensure you love your car.
05
Accessibility
Great cars shouldn't be out of reach. We offer flexible financing options and work with you to find a payment plan that fits your life.
06
Innovation
We're continuously improving — from digital inventory browsing to online enquiries, we're modernising the car buying experience for Kenya.
